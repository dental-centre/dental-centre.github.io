Appointments

We treat our patients according to the treatment plan. In general you can recieve an appointment for a periodic check within two to three weeks. During the periodic check-up, the dentist assesses the condition of your teeth and determines whether additional treatment is required.

In consultation with you, we try to ensure that the various treatment sessions are scheduled conveniently, as much as possible. We take your wishes and available time into account.

For...

  • asking general questions
  • asking questions on the medical content
  • making appointments
  • changing address or insurance details

...you can also email us at (receptie@dental-centre.nl) or send Whatsapp message at (06-58972363).



Do you want to cancel an appointment? Please do this at least 24 hours in advance.

If you are unable to attend your appointment, we ask you to inform us at least 24 hours in advance by telephone (070-3839842 - or leave a voicemail) or by email (receptie@dental-centre.nl) or by Whatsapp (06-58972363). If you do not do this on time, we may charge you costs for the reserved time.

Emergency treatments

In case of acute pain or other emergencies, call 070-3832349 during opening hours. Even if you are not registered in our practice, we will try to help you as quickly as possible. We make an appointment with you in consultation with the dentist. We strive to help you on the same day. If you are not registered in practice, you must take into account a surcharge and cash payment for emergency treatment.

If we cannot be reached by telephone during office hours, you can also leave a voicemail for emergencies. We will contact you as soon as possible.

For emergencies on Monday through Thursday outside office hours, on Friday after 12 noon and during the weekend you can also call the emergency service, telephone 070-3110305.
