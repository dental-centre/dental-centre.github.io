OUR PRACTICE

"Personal oral care and attention with a smile on our face"

At the Dental Center Voorburg we deliver personal and modern oral care within one practice! Every patient receives personal care from us that goes further than just the teeth. This also includes prevention (the prevention of cavities and gum disease), the treatment of anxious patients who receive our special attention, and the supervision of children and young people so that they too can achieve optimum oral hygiene. The personal approach is central to our team, so that we can offer you high-quality care, and we also provide it with a smile. Many patients have been coming to practice for several decades and we are therefore happy to chat to continue the long-term relationship and to know better what you, as a patient, expect from your dentist. In addition, we are transparent about the various treatment options and the associated rates.
