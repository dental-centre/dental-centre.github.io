Accessibility

By car

Dental Centre Voorburg is easily reachable by car. Plan your route here.

Parking

Within a radius of 100 meters there is almost always a parking space. You can park here for free. However, the use of a blue parking card (maximum one hour) is mandatory. We have loan parking cards available.

By public transport

The practice is very easily accessible by public transport. Plan your route from your home address with www.9292.nl

Bus

The bus stop "Van Heurnstraat" (EBS lijn 45 en 46) is at 50 meter from the practice.
The bus stop "Haagse Poort" for Connexxion Interliner 380, 381 en 382 is at three minutes walking distance.

Randstadrail

The stations "Den Haag Laan van NOI" and "Beatrixkwartier" are at 10 minutes walking distance.

Train

NS-station "Den Haag Laan van NOI" is at 10 minutes walking distance. "Den Haag Centraal Station" is easily accessible by bus, Randstadrail and train.
